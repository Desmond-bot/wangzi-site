About Us

Welcome to Wangzi, a training and skills development company. Our intentions are to afford young South Africans an opportunity to produce a career in Port Operations and qualify as a Master or Chief Engineer in Port and Coastal limitation. In order for you to be enrolled, you need to be a Maritime student who has completed all modules up to and including third year. Our success is measured by the success of our cadets. We take pride in the achievement of our cadets, each of whom has a unique success story to tell. At Wangzi, we believe in the transformative power of education. Our training programs are designed to equip our cadets with the tools they need to excel in the maritime sector.
